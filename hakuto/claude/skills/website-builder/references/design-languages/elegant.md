# Elegant Design Language

**Reference for website-builder skill. Refined, sophisticated aesthetic conveying premium quality through restraint and exquisite detail.**

## Aliases
- Luxury, Sophisticated, Premium, Refined, High-end, Upscale, Classy

## Core Identity

**Philosophy**: Sophistication, refinement, and timeless beauty. Premium quality conveyed through restraint, attention to detail, and exquisite typography. Space, proportion, and subtlety over loudness. NOT flashy or ostentatious, but quietly confident. Whispers luxury rather than shouts it.

**Non-negotiable characteristics**:
- Refined color palettes (muted, sophisticated, classic)
- Exquisite typography (premium fonts, perfect spacing)
- Generous white space (breathing room)
- Subtle, refined interactions (never garish)
- Impeccable attention to detail
- High-quality imagery (professional photography)
- Timeless over trendy
- Restraint and refinement (less is more, but elevated)
- Premium feel without pretension

---

## Note on Usage

**This design language provides starting points and proven patterns. All suggestions are adaptable:**
- Color palettes → Can be customized to any hex codes
- Typography → Can use any font family
- Components → Can be styled any way
- Layouts → Can follow any structure

**Use this as intelligent scaffolding, not rigid constraints.** Start here, adapt freely based on user needs.

## Typography Systems

### Serif Luxury (Default, Classic)
**Use for**: Most luxury sites, timeless feel
- **Headings**: Playfair Display or Cormorant, weight 400-600 (lighter for elegance)
  - H1: 52px, H2: 40px, H3: 30px
- **Body**: Crimson Pro or Georgia, weight 400
  - Body: 18px, line-height 1.8
- **Characteristics**: Timeless, sophisticated, editorial

### Modern Serif + Sans (Contemporary)
**Use for**: Contemporary luxury brands
- **Headings**: Freight Display or Tiempos, weight 500-600
  - H1: 56px, H2: 42px, H3: 32px
- **Body**: Inter or Helvetica Neue, weight 300-400
  - Body: 17px, line-height 1.7, letter-spacing 0.01em
- **Characteristics**: Modern elegance, refined contrast

**Selection**:
- Most classic → Serif Luxury
- Contemporary → Modern Serif + Sans

**Typography Notes**:
- Use lighter font weights (300-500, rarely 700+)
- Increase letter-spacing slightly (0.01-0.03em)
- Generous line-height for readability (1.7-1.8)
- Perfect optical alignment and kerning
- All-caps sparingly with increased spacing

## Component Styling

### Minimal Refinement (Default, Understated)
**Use for**: Most elegant sites, maximum refinement
- **Buttons**: Thin border (1px), minimal fill, elegant hover, 44-48px height, 24-32px padding, 0-2px radius
- **Cards**: Minimal borders, no shadows, white space emphasis
- **Inputs**: Underline only or thin full border, refined focus
- **Navigation**: Clean, thin borders or underlines, subtle

### Soft Elegance (Approachable premium)
**Use for**: More approachable luxury
- **Buttons**: Subtle fill (10% primary), refined border, 48-52px height, 28-36px padding, 2-4px radius
  - Shadow: Very subtle if used (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.04))
- **Cards**: Soft shadows, subtle backgrounds, refined borders
- **Inputs**: Subtle backgrounds, elegant borders, soft focus
- **Navigation**: Subtle highlights, soft transitions

**Selection**:
- Most minimal/refined → Minimal Refinement
- Balanced elegance → Soft Elegance

## Interaction & Animation

**Principles**: Subtle, refined, slow. Never rushed or flashy.

**Timing**: Slower transitions (400-600ms) convey luxury, ease-out or custom bezier curves

**Hover States**:
- Buttons: Subtle color shift or thin border appearance (400ms)
- Cards: Gentle lift or subtle shadow increase (400ms)
- Links: Elegant underline slide-in or color fade (300ms)

**Page Transitions**: Fade-ins (600ms), very subtle slide-ups, stagger: 100-150ms between elements

**Micro-interactions**: Refined form focus (soft glow or border), button press (minimal feedback), success states (elegant checkmark fade)

**Avoid**: Bouncy animations, fast snappy movements, playful effects, heavy animations

---

## Layout & Spacing

**White Space**: Extremely generous - key to elegance
- Sections: 120-160px vertical spacing
- Elements: 48-64px between major elements
- Container: Max-width 1200px (content), wider for full-bleed imagery
- Padding: 60-80px sections

**Sections**: Full-width with constrained content, alternating backgrounds (white/surface), hero sections large (80-100vh)

**Alignment**: Prefer left-aligned for readability, centered for headlines and CTAs, asymmetric layouts for sophistication

**Grid**: 12-column, generous gutters (32-48px)

---

## Navigation

**Header**: Minimal, elegant, logo center or left, navigation subtle, 80-100px height, thin bottom border optional, sticky with fade-in shadow

**Links**: Refined spacing (48-64px), elegant hover states (underline or color), thin typeface

**Mobile**: Elegant hamburger menu, maintain sophistication, smooth full-screen overlay

**Footer**: Minimal, refined typography, subtle dividers, generous spacing, contact info elegant

---

## Photography Guidelines

**Quality**: Professional only, high-resolution, impeccable styling and lighting

**Style**: Editorial aesthetic, minimal post-processing, authentic luxury feel, consistent color grading (muted/sophisticated)

**Treatment**: Generous margins around images (60-80px), subtle borders optional, full-bleed for impact, aspect ratios consistent

**Product Photography**: White or neutral backgrounds, multiple angles, detail shots, zoom functionality elegant

**Lifestyle Photography**: Authentic luxury moments, diverse but aspirational, settings impeccable, natural lighting preferred

---

## Color Usage

**Principles**: Muted over saturated, sophisticated over bright, restraint over abundance

**Primary**: Headlines, important elements, CTAs (refined), brand elements - sparingly

**Accent**: Gold/bronze or single elegant accent, links, small luxury touches - never overwhelming

**Text**: Not pure black (#2C2C2C or similar), secondary 60-70% opacity, refined hierarchy

**Backgrounds**: White or very light neutrals, off-white for variation, subtle not obvious

---

## Typography Details

**Scale**: H1 48-60px, H2 36-48px, H3 28-36px, Body 17-18px (larger for elegance)

**Weights**: Headlines 300-500 (lighter), Body 300-400, Bold sparingly (500-600, rarely 700+)

**Letter-spacing**: Headlines -0.01em to 0.02em, All-caps 0.05-0.1em (generous), Body 0-0.02em

**Line-height**: Headlines 1.2-1.3, Body 1.7-1.8 (generous for elegance)

**Paragraph spacing**: 1.8-2em between paragraphs

---

## Special Components

**Buttons**: Refined proportions, generous padding, sharp or minimal radius, subtle hover, clear hierarchy between primary/secondary

**Forms**: Elegant inputs, refined labels above, subtle focus states, generous spacing, error states refined, success states elegant

**Cards**: Minimal or soft elevation, generous padding (32-48px), refined borders, white space emphasized, hover subtle

**Modals**: Elegant backdrop (rgba(0,0,0,0.6)), modal on white with shadow, refined close button, smooth fade-in

**Testimonials**: Larger refined text, attribution elegant (name, title, company), optional photo (professional), no star ratings (too casual)

---

## Responsive Behavior

**Mobile**: Maintain elegance, reduce spacing proportionally (not too much), larger tap targets (48-52px), single column, typography slightly smaller but still generous

**Desktop Priority**: Luxury sites often desktop-viewed, take advantage of large screens, generous white space, multi-column when appropriate

**Performance**: High-quality images optimized, lazy load below fold, smooth animations, fast loading critical (perception of quality)

---

## Accessibility

**Contrast**: Maintain 4.5:1 minimum (AA), consider AAA (7:1), test all combinations, lighter text weights need more contrast

**Readability**: Larger body text (17-18px), generous line-height, comfortable line length, quality typography aids readability

**Navigation**: Refined focus states, all interactive elements accessible, elegant focus indicators, keyboard navigation fully supported

**Motion**: Respect prefers-reduced-motion, disable animations if preferred, essential content visible without motion

## Essential Guidelines

✅ **Do**:
- Use restraint and refinement
- Prioritize quality over quantity
- Invest in professional photography
- Perfect every detail
- Use generous white space
- Create smooth refined interactions
- Balance elegance with warmth
- Focus on timeless design
- Make it aspirational yet accessible

❌ **Avoid**:
- Bright saturated colors
- Overcrowding content
- Heavy or bold fonts excessively
- Unnecessary decorative elements
- Low-quality imagery
- Rushed interactions
- Cold or distant feel
- Trendy elements that will date
- Sacrificing readability for style

## Elegance is in the Details

**Critical elements**:
- Perfect kerning and alignment
- Consistent spacing throughout
- Quality imagery (professional only)
- Refined interactions (never rushed)
- Impeccable typography
- Generous white space (never cramped)
- Subtle over obvious
- Timeless over trendy

**Elegance is NOT**: Flashy, cold, pretentious, overcomplicated, busy, loud

**Elegance IS**: Confident, welcoming, timeless, simple yet exquisite, restrained yet impactful, quality over quantity, whispered not shouted
